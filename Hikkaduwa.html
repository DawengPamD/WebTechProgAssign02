Hikkaduwa Turtle Hatchery

About the Hatchery

The Hikkaduwa Turtle Hatchery is a conservation project located in the coastal town of Hikkaduwa in Sri Lanka. The hatchery is dedicated to protecting and preserving sea turtles by collecting their eggs, incubating them, and releasing the hatchlings back into the ocean.

Things to Do

  • Learn about sea turtles and their conservation
  • See the turtle hatchlings up close
  • Support the hatchery by adopting a turtle
  • Visit the nearby beach and go for a swim
