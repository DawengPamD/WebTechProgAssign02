Welcome to Bentota Turtle Hatchery

This is the Bentota Turtle Hatchery, where we strive to protect and conserve sea turtles.

Conservation Efforts

At our hatchery, we collect sea turtle eggs and provide a safe environment for their incubation. Once hatched, we release the baby turtles into the sea.

Visiting Us

Visitors are welcome to observe the hatchery, learn about sea turtles, and participate in the release of baby turtles.
